# 3D Models — LumenTrace

## Overview

The 3D model collection supports LumenTrace's physical infrastructure [voelkl:2026lumentrace], combining functional mechanical components with decorative environmental assets. Models are designed for 3D printing in a cohesive sci-fi aesthetic, integrating seamlessly with the racing simulation environment and audio system.
Not all of the proposed models were used in the final build, as some were replaced with alternative solutions during development or were not able to be produced due to time, resource and real-world constraints.

## Model Categories

### Mechanical Components

Functional parts required for the racing track and LED integration, including LED strip mounting fixtures, connectors, and control cable housings. Even though these components are engineered for precision fit and durability under repeated use, they were not used in the final product, as better alternatives were found and implemented.

### Environmental Decoration

Scenery and decorative structures that establish the immersive sci-fi environment surrounding the race track. These include landscape elements, architectural structures, lighting fixtures, and industrial-themed accents that enhance visual immersion without affecting gameplay.

### Audio Equipment Integration

Custom enclosures and housings for the 2.1-channel stereo speaker system, designed to complement the overall aesthetic. These cases provide protection for the audio components while ensuring optimal sound projection and integration with the track environment.

## Design and Production

All 3D-printed objects were designed in Blender, Fusion 360, TinkerCAD, retrieved from Online Sources, or generated by AI. The files are available in the folders `scad` and `stl`.
